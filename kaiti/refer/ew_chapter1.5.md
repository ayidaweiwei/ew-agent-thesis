# 1.5 Extended Warranties and Maintenance Service Contracts

# 1.5 延保与维护服务合同

---

There is lot of confusion between EWs and MSCs. To properly understand these two terms, we start with a brief discussion of base warranties.

> 延保（EW）与维护服务合同（MSC）之间存在大量混淆。为了正确理解这两个术语，我们首先简要讨论基本保修。

---

## 1.5.1 Base Warranties

## 1.5.1 基本保修

A base warranty (BW) is often simply called a warranty and is normally associated with products. It is a contractual agreement between a manufacturer and a buyer (customer) that is entered into upon the sale of a product. The contract defines the compensation available to the buyer if the performance of the product is found to be unsatisfactory. It is part of the sale, and its cost is factored into the price of the product.

> 基本保修（BW）通常简称为保修，通常与产品相关联。它是制造商与买方（客户）之间在产品销售时签订的合同协议。该合同规定了当产品性能不令人满意时，买方可获得的补偿。它是销售的一部分，其成本已计入产品价格中。

---

## 1.5.2 Extended Warranties

## 1.5.2 延长保修

An EW is associated mainly with standard (consumer, industrial and commercial) products. The difference between a BW and an EW is that the latter is entered into voluntarily and is purchased separately—the buyer may even have a choice of terms, whereas a BW is part of product purchase and integral to the sale. The terms of an EW can be the same as those for the BW provided by the manufacturer, or they may differ in the sense that the EW may include additional features such as

- Cost sharing,
- Exclusions,
- Cost limits on individual claims or on the total claims made under the warranty,
- Deductibles.

> 延保主要与标准产品（消费类、工业类和商业类）相关。基本保修（BW）与延保（EW）的区别在于：后者是自愿签订并单独购买的——买方甚至可以选择条款，而基本保修是产品购买的一部分，与销售不可分割。延保的条款可以与制造商提供的基本保修相同，也可以不同，延保可能包含以下附加特征：
>
> - 费用分摊
> - 免责条款
> - 单次索赔或保修期内总索赔的费用上限
> - 免赔额

EWs are currently offered on a wide range of products, including automobiles, electronics, appliances and many other items. Often the customer has to buy an EW at the same time as the product is purchased. In some cases, the customer has the option to buy an EW any time before the BW expires or even after the BW expiry date. The customer pays an extra amount, depending on the duration and the terms of the EW.

> 延保目前适用于广泛的产品，包括汽车、电子产品、家用电器和许多其他物品。通常客户必须在购买产品的同时购买延保。在某些情况下，客户可以在基本保修到期前甚至到期后的任何时间购买延保。客户需要支付额外费用，具体金额取决于延保的期限和条款。

Originally, EWs were offered only by manufacturers. Currently, for some products, they are offered by other service providers (such as retailers, insurance companies and other parties) rather than by manufacturers. For other products, manufacturers compete with the EW service providers. Two important features of EWs are the following:

- The terms of an EW are decided by the manufacturer or service provider with the customer having limited input.
- An EW covers only the CM costs (associated with failures of components that are not excluded) but not the PM costs or any consequential losses incurred by the owner of the product.

> 最初，延保仅由制造商提供。目前，对于某些产品，延保由其他服务提供商（如零售商、保险公司和其他方）而非制造商提供。对于其他产品，制造商与延保服务提供商之间存在竞争。延保的两个重要特征如下：
>
> - 延保条款由制造商或服务提供商决定，客户的参与度有限。
> - 延保仅覆盖纠正性维护（CM）成本（与未被排除的组件故障相关），不包括预防性维护（PM）成本或产品所有者遭受的任何间接损失。

---

## 1.5.3 Maintenance Service Contracts

## 1.5.3 维护服务合同

A MSC is similar to EW in that the maintenance of a system is carried out by an external service provider. The MSC defines the period over which the maintenance (PM and/or CM) actions are to be carried out and the payment to be made by the owner of the system to the service provider. These contracts are often associated with the maintenance of products (commercial and industrial), plants and infrastructures.

> 维护服务合同（MSC）与延保类似，即系统的维护由外部服务提供商执行。MSC 规定了维护（预防性维护 PM 和/或纠正性维护 CM）行动的执行期限，以及系统所有者向服务提供商支付的费用。这些合同通常与产品（商业和工业类）、工厂和基础设施的维护相关。

MSCs can be either (1) standard contracts offered by the service provider, (2) contracts where some customisation of a standard contract is made to meet the specific needs of an owner and (3) contracts initiated and dictated by the owner. Standard (and customised) contracts are mainly for standard products. Owner initiated contracts are usually for the maintenance of complex systems and infrastructures with one or more service providers.

> MSC 可以是：（1）服务提供商提供的标准合同；（2）对标准合同进行一定定制以满足所有者特定需求的合同；（3）由所有者发起和主导的合同。标准（和定制）合同主要适用于标准产品。所有者发起的合同通常用于与一个或多个服务提供商合作维护复杂系统和基础设施。

MSCs can include none, or one or more of the following:

- PM only, CM only or both PM and CM,
- Exclusions,
- Deductibles,
- Cost limits,
- Guarantees on system or service performance,
- Incentives (penalties) if the system or service performance exceeds (falls below) a specified level,
- Compensation for some or all of the consequential losses incurred by the owner due to the unavailability of the product (system).

> MSC 可以不包含或包含以下一项或多项内容：
>
> - 仅 PM、仅 CM 或 PM 和 CM 兼有
> - 免责条款
> - 免赔额
> - 费用上限
> - 系统或服务性能保证
> - 当系统或服务性能超过（低于）规定水平时的激励（惩罚）
> - 因产品（系统）不可用而给所有者造成的部分或全部间接损失的补偿
